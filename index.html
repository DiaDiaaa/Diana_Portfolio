Solo Project

CrispGameLib

2023

In the dynamic world of game development, my journey took an exciting turn during a rapid prototype course, where the idea of creating a beatbox system triggered by mouse clicks emerged. This concept was born out of an inspiring brainstorming session with my teammates, drawing parallels between a captivating game and a music visualizer I had coded in the Summer of 2020. The vision further evolved with inspiration from a colleague's artistic project involving magnetic fields and mouse interaction. This fusion led to the conception of a unified, one-button game that incorporated these diverse elements, promising an innovative and immersive player experience.

Playthrough Presentation

Play

The central query driving my prototype was straightforward yet ambitious: "How can a one-button game harmoniously integrate elements from a game, a music visualizer, and a colleague's magnetic-field project?" The answer lay in the prototype's revelation. Integration transformed functionality and user experience. Elements from the game introduced engaging interaction, letting players intentionally contribute to the beat. The music visualizer elements brought dynamic and responsive visual feedback, syncing with the rhythm. Inspired by my colleague's magnetic fields, the game added a captivating visual intensity, creating an immersive and visually stimulating experience. The outcome was not just a game; it was a harmonious blend where a single click orchestrated a symphony.

Yet, the journey to harmonize these inspirations into a one-button game wasn't without challenges. Positioning arrow characters on the screen became a nuanced struggle, demanding refinement for a seamless player experience. An unexpected realization dawned during implementation—I yearned to enhance visualization further with particles. However, limitations within the current library prompted me to explore alternative solutions and libraries to align the game closer with my vision.

"One Button Harmony" evolved through revisions, transforming into a green light-red light dynamic. A click on yellow and green lights brought points, while the red light deducted them. To elevate this one-button symphony, I am committed to refining the arrow character positioning, ensuring an intuitive and fluid user interaction. My dedication to exploring advanced particle effects aims to augment the visual experience, immersing players deeper into the harmonious world created by a simple, yet powerful, click.

Play Game

Game Designer, Storytelling

Inky

2022

"A Wonderful Week" is a horror dating simulator, immersing players in the role of a psychologist treating Jennifer, who struggles with Dissociative Identity Disorder (DID). The game delves into a complex narrative where the player must navigate the delicate balance of earning trust and favorability from Jennifer's dual personalities—a benevolent side and a malevolent devil side. As the psychologist, you must unravel hidden truths, all while avoiding the peril of the devil side's murderous intentions. The game culminates in various endings based on the player's choices and the level of trust cultivated with Jennifer.



The game adopts a Branch and Bottleneck design pattern, allowing events to branch based on player choices, variables, and states. This dynamic structure, illustrated through a Twine graph, ensures a varied narrative experience with multiple possible endings.

To enhance the narrative, we employed CSS formatting in the HTML version, assigning colors to characters for clarity. Easter eggs and hidden messages, represented by different colors, add intrigue. In Cthulhu-inspired elements, we integrated R'lyeh language, symbols in the commercial list, and interactive fourth-wall-breaking moments with Hastur, creating a unique and immersive experience.

In "Psychological Odyssey: Unraveling Minds," the fusion of narrative intricacies, dynamic choices, and immersive elements promises players an enthralling journey through the realms of psychology, suspense, and the unknown. Will you decipher the mysteries or succumb to the madness that lurks within? The answers lie in the choices you make on this psychological odyssey.

SCP Escape

Play Game

Solo Project

Twine

2021

Embark on a heart-pounding journey with "SCP Escape," a decrypt and escape game set within the SCP Foundation prison. As a vulnerable class-D member, you find yourself trapped amidst anti-human SCP characters during a chaotic breakout. Navigate the dangerous asylum, interacting with SCP characters, making crucial decisions, and solving puzzles to secure your safe escape.

During the planning session, I, along with my friend Zixin, a passionate SCP lover, meticulously mapped out the game. We strategically placed locations and interconnections within the SCP Foundation prison, guiding the distinct branch rejoin hyperstructure. This collaboration allowed players to explore various approaches, fostering decision-making integral to the escape narrative.



The game utilizes links for navigation and conversation choices with guiding SCP characters. Encounter potentially harmful characters such as SCP 173, SCP 096, SCP 939, SCP 682, SCP 053, SCP 0294, SCP 035, SCP 073, SCP 953, SCP 847, SCP 860, and SCP 458. Choices dictate outcomes—earn time to dodge or face an immediate bad ending. This link structure adds dynamism, providing players agency in survival scenarios.

The "Distinct Branch Rejoin" hyperstructure aligns with the game's link structure. Nodes, organized based on the SCP Foundation prison's geography, enable varied approaches, fostering exploration and decision-making integral to the escape narrative. In "SCP Escape," players are thrust into the heart of SCP Foundation chaos, where every choice matters. Decrypt mysteries, navigate dangers, and emerge victorious in this intense escape narrative. The choice is yours in the dark corridors of the asylum.

Solitario

Solo Project

Game Poster

2020

The overall design concept draws inspiration from the lighthearted nature of Solitaire, aiming to convey a sense of sweetness and playful entertainment. The combination of fonts, Baloo and Montserrat, contributes to this by blending round edges for a playful look (Baloo) with straight edges for visual contrast (Montserrat).

A carefully chosen color palette enhances the theme of leisure and entertainment. Lava and beau blue serve as main colors, creating a harmonious and inviting atmosphere. Light steel blue and queen pink gradients in the background emphasize a cool afternoon and sparkling water feeling, overall imparting a soft and chill sensation to the poster.


Taking the same game, Solitaire, the interactive poster ideation introduces a simplified yet engaging approach. Nine cubes, representing the letters in "Solitaire," use a candy color palette, providing a visually appealing and vibrant display. The interactive journey unfolds with cursor movement triggering dynamic color changes in the cubes. Pressing a cube reveals the corresponding letter ("S") with accompanying minion sound effects, mimicking the tactile experience of pressing buttons on a phone dial pad. Additional interaction includes pressing cubes corresponding to the letter "I," unveiling little figures subtly hinting at Solitaire being a card game. The culmination involves pressing all letters or cubes, bringing two little figures together, with remaining information appearing to conclude the interactive poster experience.

The interactive poster ideation serves as a simplified base, allowing for potential expansion with additional elements like animations and dialogues between figures, contributing to a more enriched and engaging visual narrative.

Tom and Jerry Board Game

Play Game

Game Designer, Artist

Board Game

2020

Tom and Jerry is a long-running American cartoon series revolving around Tom Cat’s attempts to capture Jerry Mouse. Chaos ensues and Tom’s plans are thwarted by Jerry’s wits. Sometimes the mayhem extends to the annoyance of Spike Bulldog or the housemaid Mrs. Two-Shoes, in which either would come to punish Tom. Throughout the years, the series applied its plot formula in a number of settings including the house, medieval France, and even outer space.

The game instills excitement and tension as players emulate Tom's pursuit of Jerry. The pursuit and evasion mechanics capture the chaotic and humorous chases seen in the cartoon.

Players engage in strategic decision-making, deciding when to deploy item cards for offense or defense. This adds unpredictability, mirroring the unexpected twists in the cartoon.

The game encourages engaging interactions among players. Item card usage and strategic positioning create a dynamic environment, reflecting the slapstick and interactive nature of the cartoon.

The uncertainty in item card usage introduces surprise, requiring players to adapt. This mirrors the unforeseen predicaments often faced by Tom and Jerry, adding humor and unpredictability.

Playing as Jerry, Tom, or the additional challenge of Spike instills a desire for victory. The competitive drive, coupled with thematic elements, immerses players in the world of "Tom and Jerry," where wit and strategy lead to victory.

"Tom and Jerry: The Board Game" delivers beyond a simple adaptation, providing an interactive experience that encapsulates the humor, chaos, and competitive spirit of the iconic cartoon. The game offers a unique blend of strategy, unpredictability, and player interaction, ensuring an entertaining and emotionally engaging gaming session.
