Phaser

2022

Embark on an immersive adventure with Dreamchaser, a captivating puzzle platformer that takes players on a profound journey through the realms of dreams and memories. This game offers a unique and engaging experience that combines compelling gameplay mechanics, a rich narrative, and visually stunning art styles.

Navigate dreamscapes, collect memories, and reclaim what's lost. Run, jump, change forms, and use a dreamcatcher. Face enemies and spikes, strategically collect hearts, or choose a more challenging path by avoiding them.

Diverse worlds, each with a distinct color palette. Level 1 in stark white portrays confusion, Level 2 in vibrant greens and yellows transforms into a jungle, and Level 3's desert signals the narrative climax.

Your journey begins in the Dream Therapist's office, where I personally guided the framework for a deeply immersive narrative. Dark, dream-like music sets the tone, and your visual contributions, along with the experimental effects, bring characters and elements to life, adding a layer of personal connection to the dreamworld.

I handled character design, titles, and props. This personal touch enhances the overall aesthetic, creating a visually compelling and unique identity for Dreamchaser.

In addition to character design, I personally curated experimental visual effects, enriching the game with a visually dynamic and experimental edge. Your journey through Dreamchaser will be a feast for the senses, with every detail thoughtfully considered.

Challenge yourself while enjoying a flexible experience. Restart levels or the entire game without frustration. Explore, discover, and chase your dreams in a game tailored for you.

"Where Is My Body" is more than just a game; it's an immersive experience that challenges players to navigate an endless runner world with a unique twist. As the mind behind the character art framework and the architect of the story, I ensured that every element resonates with a personal touch.

The heartbeat of the game lies in its characters. I meticulously designed the general character art framework, infusing personality and charm into each element. From the protagonist to the supporting cast, every character is a reflection of my creative vision.

A game without a story is like a body without a soul. In "Where Is My Body," I constructed a narrative that adds depth and purpose to the endless runner experience. The story unfolds seamlessly, creating an emotional connection that keeps players engaged as they run through the ever-changing landscape.

The technical backbone of "Where Is My Body" is powered by Phaser, a versatile and powerful game development framework. Leveraging my expertise, I harnessed Phaser's capabilities to create a seamless and enjoyable gaming experience.

CrispGameLib

2023

In the dynamic world of game development, my journey took an exciting turn during a rapid prototype course, where the idea of creating a beatbox system triggered by mouse clicks emerged. This concept was born out of an inspiring brainstorming session with my teammates, drawing parallels between a captivating game and a music visualizer I had coded in the Summer of 2020. The vision further evolved with inspiration from a colleague's artistic project involving magnetic fields and mouse interaction. This fusion led to the conception of a unified, one-button game that incorporated these diverse elements, promising an innovative and immersive player experience.

The central query driving my prototype was straightforward yet ambitious: "How can a one-button game harmoniously integrate elements from a game, a music visualizer, and a colleague's magnetic-field project?" The answer lay in the prototype's revelation. Integration transformed functionality and user experience. Elements from the game introduced engaging interaction, letting players intentionally contribute to the beat. The music visualizer elements brought dynamic and responsive visual feedback, syncing with the rhythm. Inspired by my colleague's magnetic fields, the game added a captivating visual intensity, creating an immersive and visually stimulating experience. The outcome was not just a game; it was a harmonious blend where a single click orchestrated a symphony.

Yet, the journey to harmonize these inspirations into a one-button game wasn't without challenges. Positioning arrow characters on the screen became a nuanced struggle, demanding refinement for a seamless player experience. An unexpected realization dawned during implementation—I yearned to enhance visualization further with particles. However, limitations within the current library prompted me to explore alternative solutions and libraries to align the game closer with my vision.

"One Button Harmony" evolved through revisions, transforming into a green light-red light dynamic. A click on yellow and green lights brought points, while the red light deducted them. To elevate this one-button symphony, I am committed to refining the arrow character positioning, ensuring an intuitive and fluid user interaction. My dedication to exploring advanced particle effects aims to augment the visual experience, immersing players deeper into the harmonious world created by a simple, yet powerful, click.
