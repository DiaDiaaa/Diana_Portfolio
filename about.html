Diana Yee

Game Designer, Game developer, Story Designer

Diana dedicated to exploring psychological interactions and understanding unique emotional needs. In my work, I emphasize that the end of a story is not the destination but the beginning of the next journey. Each of my games has its own merits, showcasing diverse styles and offering unique emotional experiences. Through this portfolio, I aim to guide players in exploring profound psychological interactions and experiencing the delicate representation of special emotional needs in the gaming world. I hope you can discover everyone's unique story within my creations.
